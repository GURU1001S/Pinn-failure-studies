Graphical Abstract Concept for "An Atlas of PINN Failures: The Zugzwang Thesis"

Dimensions: 531 x 1328 pixels (min) to 1200 x 3000 pixels (Elsevier standard aspect ratio 1:2.5 or 5:12)

Visual Layout:
A tripartite horizontal or vertical diagram illustrating the "Zugzwang Condition" of PINNs under stiffness.

1. Left Panel (The Cause): 
   A stiff PDE domain (e.g., steep Burgers shock or high-frequency advection) entering a standard MLP PINN architecture. 

2. Center Panel (The Pathology - "The Atlas"):
   A split visualization of the three core failure mechanisms identified in the paper:
   - Spectral Bias (a frequency plot showing NTK cutoff)
   - Optimization Trap (a sharp loss basin vs flat basin)
   - Data Illusions (a collocation starvation cliff)
   Arrows should show these are independent but co-occurring mechanisms.

3. Right Panel (The Consequence):
   A bar chart contrasting pointwise L2 Error (which might look artificially low) against a massive red bar for Mass Conservation Violation ($C_1$), highlighting that scalar metrics hide physical failure. 
   Text overlay: "The Zugzwang Condition: Multiple concurrent bottlenecks with no single remedy."
